The first Golang and Cloud-Native Meetup will take place on Friday, 8th of February 2019 starting 19:00 at Basislager Leipzig, ground floor.

Are you using Go? Are you curious about Go? Are you a cloud architect? You are working on an interesting project that you would like to share? Or maybe you just want to meet some fellow Gophers in your city? Then, by all means, consider dropping by.

This will be our first meeting and there will be plenty of room to participate and shape this meetup going forward.

We already have a GitHub account at https://github.com/golang-leipzig. Further sites will be set up shortly.
