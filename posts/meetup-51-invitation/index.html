For meetup #51 on Tuesday May 27, 2025 19:00 CET at Gridfuse we have a short talk/experience report by Martin Czygan on using large language models for writing and editing Go (and other) code.

Beyond Benchmarks: Human x LLM for Go and other code (intermediate report)

Writing a prompt is like researching a model. Since 01/2025 I have been conducting a self-experiment trying to integrate various LLMs into Go software development (and also other languages), with mixed results. This short talk gives a summary with examples, findings, hits and misses.
