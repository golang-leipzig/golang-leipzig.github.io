Free Form Meetup #37

Since starting Leipzig Gophers almost four years ago we always aimed at connecting people and to learn about interesting Go projects and we had a lot of fun organizing that. Meetup #37 on 2023-09-26 aimed to be a free-form event, that is an event without a defined agenda with an opportunity to meet and discuss.

We’ll try to sharpen the free-form event format in the future.
