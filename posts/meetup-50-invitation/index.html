For meetup #50 on Tuesday Apr 29, 2025 19:00 CET at Basislager Coworking we are thrilled to have two input presentations:

How a Go project dies; lessons learned, challenges to revive it and the scars I got on the way

  • Simon will take another closer look at StackRox, the Kubernetes Security platform. He’ll be talking about the client-server protocol between the StackRox server and its agent and discuss how to maintain backwards compatibility, manage multiple event streams from clusters and how the authentication works.

Join us for retrospectives and real-world software project insights!
