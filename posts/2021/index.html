🔗2021

Dec 29, 2021
Tags 2021

Unfortunately, most of the meetups were remote-only again in 2021 - however, on the plus side:

  • we started the year with a new Leipzig Gopher mascot - thanks @klingtnet
  • we grew from 194 to 324 members in just eleven months 📈
  • we had nine events, eight online, one offline (#20)
  • we had presentations from two German startup companies (#22, #23)
  • we started a YouTube channel (thanks @embano1), recorded three great talks (18 subscribers, 330+ views) 📹
  • we had a first (X-mas 🎄) quiz night (#24) on Go and some Go gotchas and found great sponsors like Jetbrains, O’Reilly Media, Manning Publications and About You (jobs) 🎁
  • we continued to have people joining from around the world, which is incredible 🌐
  • we had a lovely time hosting the meetup and hope you had that too

Thanks everyone for participating, we hope to see you again in 2022!
