We would like to invite you to a special hackathon event this year: Hackolaus #2 - running from Nov 19 to Nov 21, 2021 at Basislager Leipzig.

When the nights get longer and the weather outside is frightful, we need some warmth in our hearts. Many people sacrifice their time and energy to create this warmth by giving and contributing to the benefit of those less fortunate and in need.

Many NGOs, associations and individuals also work hard to give to social, environmental or other sustainability causes. We believe that they should not only give but also receive, that society should give back to them.

This is why we are collaborating on a hackathon to support these organizations with solutions to help them focus more on delivering impact.

Go is a wonderful language for prototyping and a cross-compiled binary can be the sweetest gift, if it solves a small problem and saves someone some time.
