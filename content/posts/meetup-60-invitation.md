## Learn Go and NixOS deployments with large language models

Hello, 世界!

When used sensibly, language models can be tutors, too. For meetup #60 on
Tuesday [May 26, 2026 19:00
CET](https://www.meetup.com/leipzig-golang/events/312537727) we are really
happy to have [Maxime](https://www.linkedin.com/in/plumps/) dive into Nix/NixOS
with the help of language models.

Nix is a functional package manager and its immutable traits enable robust,
reproducable deployments of Go (and other) projects. The high level goals of
Nix/NixOS are similar to other immutable Linux distributions, like
[Silverblue](https://fedoraproject.org/silverblue/) or
[Bazzite](https://bazzite.gg/).

Becoming familiar with the Nix configuration language can be a challenge, but
one that a well instructed tutor may help with.

> Artificial intelligence techniques are increasingly used in education to
> enable personalized learning and intelligent tutoring [LLM Agents for
> Education: Advances and Applications](https://aclanthology.org/anthology-files/anthology-files/pdf/findings/2025.findings-emnlp.743.pdf) (11/2025)
